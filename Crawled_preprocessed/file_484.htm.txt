in advisor insights log in newsletters evening star definition of evening star an evening star is a bearish candlestick pattern consisting of three candles that have demonstrated the following characteristics the first bar is a large white candlestick located within an uptrend the middle bar is a small bodied candle red or white that closes above the first white bar and the last bar is a large red candle that opens below the middle candle and closes near the center of the first bar s body this pattern is used by traders as an early indication the uptrend is about to reverse breaking
