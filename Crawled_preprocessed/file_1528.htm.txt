the first things on the buyer s mind but it should be while all cars have a manufacturer s warranty not all auto manufacturer warranties are created equal when purchasing a new car we all want and believe that our car is going to have a long healthy life but things happen transmissions fails motors die and if these parts are not covered under a manufacturer s warranty the car buyer is the one that will be footing the bill before you buy your next car it is wise to research which car manufacturers offer the most generous warranties here is a look at five of the best auto manufacturer warranties in the market hyundaione of the most generous auto manufacturer warranties available is offered by hyundai the south korean automotive giant offers a hearty manufacturer s warranty with a five year mile basic warranty in addition they offer a year mile power train warranty a seven year corrosion warranty with an unlimited amount of miles and a five year road assistance hyundai also offers a lifetime hybrid battery warranty and a seven year anti perforation warranty hyundai describes their manufacturer warranty as america s best warranty and when compared side by side with other auto manufacturer warranties it is clear that they are not stretching the truth mitsubishimitsubishi offers a manufacturer warranty that is highly comparable to hyundai s the japanese auto maker boasts a five year mile new vehicle limited warranty in addition they offer a year mile power train warranty a five year mile warranty on the restraint system and a seven year mile anti corrosion perforation warranty also included with their warranties are adjustment coverage for months and miles and roadside assistance for five years kiaanother auto manufacturer that offers a solid manufacturer warranty is kia the south korean auto manufacturer offers a five year mile limited basic warranty along with a year mile power train warranty a five year mile limited anti perforation warranty and five year mile roadside assistance kia largely matches hyundai and mitsubishi s highly praised manufacturer warranties in all areas except for the mile limitation on the roadside assistance all in all kia offers one of the most attractive warranties on the market volvovolvo offers a highly attractive new vehicle warranty the swedish auto maker offers a four year mile warranty on all new vehicles and well as a barrage of vehicle protection such as a year mile battery warranty two year genuine volvo replacement parts warranty one year adjustment warranty a year corrosion protection warranty and more the swedish auto giant has long been appreciated for its standards in vehicle safety but their warranty is not too shabby either rolls roycerolls royce an english luxury car maker offers a highly desirable manufacturer s warranty with a four year unlimited mileage warranty in addition to the standard warranty rolls royce also offers four years of service and maintenance and four years of roadside assistance the bottom linewith all the money that is spent on cars you want to make sure that the vehicle you purchase is backed up by a solid manufacturer warranty a solid manufacturer warranty will not only save you big money should something go wrong it also gives you a glimpse of what the company thinks about their clientele by providing a top notch
