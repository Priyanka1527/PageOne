Course Description 

Investopedia Academy has partnered with Lex Sokolin— a fintech entrepreneur and cryptocurrency strategist—to provide you with the skill set required to understand and navigate the booming cryptocurrency market.

This course is your entry point to the world of cryptocurrency. You’ll start with the basic terminology, move to the fundamentals of blockchain and mining, and finish with the knowledge you need to buy, sell and exchange Bitcoin, Ethereum, Ripple and other cryptocurrencies.

You’ll learn:

  • The history of cryptocurrency: where we are today and how we got here
  • The importance of the blockchain, wallets, and the ecosystem
  • How to buy and sell cryptocurrencies, while mitigating risk
  • The basics of initial coin offerings (ICO’s) and ICO investment strategies
  • The future of cryptocurrency: where we’ll be tomorrow and how to get there first

This course includes:

  • Over 90 minutes of on-demand video, exercises, and interactive content
  • Lifetime access to every video in the course

By taking Cryptocurrency for Beginners today, you will learn to make decisions about the Bitcoin ecosystem using your new knowledge, analytical frameworks, and practical step-by-step guides.  Lex is a leader in the fintech world and is passionate about sharing his knowledge with people entering the cryptocurrency universe.
